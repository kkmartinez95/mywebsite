About Me

Originally from The Bronx, I am a Yankee fan by default and a dog-mom to my two Chihuahuas, Bridgette and Sophie. At the beginning of 2019, I was a coffee barista, office assistant and event coordinator and a fulltime student at CUNY New York College of Technology majoring in Radiology. Upon chance, I came across Fullstack's WDF program, applied, was accepted, graduated, and later selected for Fullstack's teaching fellowship for the current Grace Hopper cohort!

I realized I wanted to have a career where I have creative expression and an environment meant for constant learning. Creating, teaching, and helping others to understand the limitless possiblities of coding is fascinating!

If you don't find me coding, I would be playing with my pups, solving crossword puzzles and watching (and singing along to) Disney movies.
